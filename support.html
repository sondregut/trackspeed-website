Frequently Asked Questions

How does TrackSpeed work?

TrackSpeed uses your iPhone's camera at 240 frames per second to detect when an athlete crosses the timing gate. The app uses advanced pose detection to identify when the athlete's chest crosses the virtual finish line, providing millisecond-accurate timing.

Do I need special equipment?

No! TrackSpeed works with just your iPhone. For split timing or start/finish gates, you can use two iPhones that sync together wirelessly.

What iPhone models are supported?

TrackSpeed requires an iPhone with 240fps slow-motion capability. This includes iPhone 6 and later models. For best performance, we recommend iPhone 12 or newer.

How accurate is the timing?

At 240fps, TrackSpeed can detect crossings with approximately 4-millisecond precision. When using two devices, our clock synchronization ensures both gates are aligned to within 1 millisecond.

Can I use TrackSpeed for official competitions?

TrackSpeed is designed for training and unofficial timing. For official competitions, please use certified timing equipment as required by your sport's governing body.

How do I connect two phones together?

Both devices need to have TrackSpeed installed. One device creates a session as the "host," and the other joins. The devices connect automatically via peer-to-peer networking - no internet connection required.

Why does the app need camera access?

The camera is essential for detecting when athletes cross the timing gate. TrackSpeed processes video frames in real-time to identify crossings. Video is processed on-device and is not uploaded or stored unless you explicitly save a session.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find TrackSpeed and tap it
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Troubleshooting

The app isn't detecting crossings

  • Ensure good lighting - the camera needs to clearly see the athlete
  • Position the phone so the athlete passes perpendicular to the camera view
  • Make sure the athlete's torso is visible in the frame
  • Try adjusting the detection sensitivity in settings

Two devices won't connect

  • Ensure both devices have WiFi and Bluetooth enabled
  • Both devices should be within 30 feet of each other
  • Try closing and reopening the app on both devices
  • Make sure both devices are running the latest version of TrackSpeed

The app is running slow or getting hot

Processing 240fps video is intensive. If your device is getting warm:

  • Remove any phone case to help with cooling
  • Avoid direct sunlight on the device
  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Take breaks between timing sessions
